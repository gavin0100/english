3. Tell me something about the weather in your home town. If I want to visit, when is the best time to come?

**Outline**:
Weather → Detail → Best time → Reason

```
=> Well, the weather in my hometown, Lam Dong, is generally quite cool and pleasant compared to big cities. It’s known for its fresh air and mild temperature, which makes it very comfortable to live in.

There are basically two main seasons, the rainy season and the dry season, and it can get quite wet during the rainy months.

If you want to visit, I would recommend going in the dry season, especially from around November to April, because the weather is much nicer and more suitable for outdoor activities.
```

